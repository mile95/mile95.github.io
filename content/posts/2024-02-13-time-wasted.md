I can’t pinpoint the exact moment I first encountered the quote _'The time you enjoy wasting is not wasted time,'_ but it has taken up residence in my mind, rent-free, ever since.

To me, this quote underscores the significance of engaging in activities that bring joy. 
If, in the end, these activities leave you happier, then the time invested is far from wasted.
The concept of wasting time is subjective; what may seem unproductive to one person might be a source of fulfillment for another.

I’m happy this quote is living rent free in my brain. It serves as a valuable reminder to prioritize activities that bring me joy, irrespective of external opinions.

So, embrace the idea of investing time in things that bring you joy! After all, as long as you find enjoyment in it, it's time well spent.

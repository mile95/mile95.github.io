Advent of code 2023 was something I really enjoyed.
I managed to collect 40 out of 50 stars before new years which is my best year so far.
I even consider going back and finishing the last 10 stars... We will see.


I live in Sweden and the local time when the puzzles get released is 6am.
My plan this year was to wake up just before 6 and try to solve the daily puzzle before 7:30 and continue during the evening if needed.
If I would not finish the daily puzzle during the day, I would not continue with this puzzle the next day.
A new day meant a new puzzle, even if I did not finish the day before.
I think this was the key to why I continued for the whole December, previusly I've lost motivation half-way.
Probably because I did not move on from "failures"
